# 🛍️ Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Dashboard

An Excel-based data analytics project that analyzes sales performance of **Vrinda Store** and provides actionable insights to improve business growth.

The project uses **Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, and Slicers)** to transform raw sales data into an interactive dashboard for better decision-making.

---

# 📊 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze sales data and identify:

- Customer purchasing patterns
- Sales performance across states
- Gender and age group buying behavior
- Contribution of different sales channels
- Monthly order vs sales trends

The final output is an **interactive Excel dashboard** that helps stakeholders easily explore key business metrics.

---

# 🧰 Tools & Technologies Used

- Microsoft Excel

- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization
- Interactive Slicers
- Charts & Dashboards

---

# 📈 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes the following visual insights:

### Order vs Sales Trend
Shows monthly order count and total sales performance.

### Men vs Women Sales
Analyzes gender-based purchasing behavior.

### Order Status
Displays distribution of order delivery status including:
- Delivered
- Cancelled
- Returned
- Refunded

### Top 5 State Sales
Highlights the top-performing states based on total sales.

### Age vs Gender Orders
Shows purchasing trends across different age groups.

### Sales Channels
Analyzes contribution of various online platforms such as:
- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra
- Ajio
- Meesho

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Women customers contribute approximately **65% of total purchases**.
- **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** are the top 3 performing states (~35% of sales).
- The **Adult age group (30–49 years)** contributes the highest share (~50%).
- **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** account for nearly **80% of total sales channels**.

---

# 🎯 Business Recommendation

To increase sales, Vrinda Store should:

- Focus marketing efforts on **women customers aged 30–49 years**.
- Target high-performing states such as **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**.
- Promote products using **ads, offers, and coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra**.

This targeted marketing strategy can significantly improve sales performance.
